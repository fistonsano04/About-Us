BAG

Our Mission:

Our mission at BAG is to identify, train, and empower the next generation of African software developers, equipping them with the skills, mentorship, and opportunities they need to excel in the tech industry. We are committed to nurturing talent and driving innovation, contributing to the growth and advancement of Africa's technology ecosystem.

Our Vision:

At BAG, our vision is to create a thriving community of highly skilled and globally competitive software developers in Rwanda and across Africa. We aspire to be a catalyst for positive change, where our graduates become leaders and innovators in the tech world, shaping the future of technology on the African continent and beyond.
